About Us

FoodYield is designed to reduce food wastage and use it in a useful way. This system also provides a platform for people to donate food. We are in contact with 150+ banquets. We collect the donated food and distribute it among people who need it. It not only reduces food waste but also alleviates hunger, a major problem in the world. We believe nutritious food should go to people, not landfills.

Our Goal

• Promote food donation practices to help reduce food wastage.
• Provide a simple and accessible platform for organizations and individuals to connect.
• Raise awareness about the importance of reducing food waste in our communities.
• Ensure transparency and trust in the donation process through real-time monitoring and reporting.

Our Mission

Our mission is to minimize food wastage by facilitating the donation of excess food from hotels, events, and organizations to individuals, orphanages, and communities that need it most. Through our platform, we aim to improve food security and reduce the environmental impact of food waste.

Our Approach

We bring together donors and recipients through a streamlined platform. Our website provides a space for organizations to offer excess food, for individuals to request donations, and for associations to monitor and analyze food distribution and waste data. This approach enables efficient food management and supports local communities in need.
